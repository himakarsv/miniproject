ABOUT THE PROJECT

MOTIVE

The main motive behind this project is to help farmers financially. Farmers sell their products or yeilds at a cost that will be set by the intermediate persons who buys the yeild. By this farmers don't get the profits they deserve. This platform helps farmers to get their actual profits

FARMER'S BENEFIT

Through this platform farmers can get their deserved profits. Farmer will be the only price regulator for the yeilds. No intermediate person will interfere as there will be a direct connection between the farmers and customers. This also helps in the economical growth of the country

USER'S BENEFITS

As users we do also get a lot of benefits. Since the price regulation is done only by the farmers, we get the products at much cheaper price than earlier. No need of worrying about more increase in prices because farmers sell their products in the MSP range itself. We can also get fresh products since we buy directly from the farmers.
